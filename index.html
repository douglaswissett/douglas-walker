About me

I have over two years of industry experience and am a Web Development Immersive graduate of General Assembly New York. I have been part of design and development projects throughout my experiences in the field.


I specialize in standards-based XHTML, CSS and JavaScript web development. Each site I develop is built to be search engine friendly, using the latest development principes and techniques.
